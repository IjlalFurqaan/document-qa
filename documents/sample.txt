ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (2025 Edition)
====================================================

1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
-------------------
ACME Corporation is a global technology company founded in 2005, headquartered in San Francisco, California. We specialize in enterprise software solutions, cloud infrastructure, and artificial intelligence services. With over 12,000 employees across 30 countries, ACME is committed to innovation, diversity, and sustainable growth.

Our mission: "To empower organizations worldwide with intelligent technology that drives efficiency, creativity, and human potential."

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
----------------------
ACME supports a flexible hybrid work model. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval. Key guidelines include:

- Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM in your local time zone.
- All remote work must comply with data security policies (see Section 7).
- Employees must maintain a dedicated, secure workspace at home.
- VPN must be connected at all times when accessing internal systems remotely.
- International remote work requires prior HR approval and may have tax implications.
- Equipment stipend: $500 annually for home office setup.

Full-time remote positions are available for select roles. Contact HR for eligibility.

3. LEAVE POLICY
---------------
ACME provides the following leave benefits to full-time employees:

- Annual Leave: 20 days per calendar year (prorated for new hires)
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year (unused days do not carry over)
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary
- Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family
- Mental Health Days: 4 additional days per year (no documentation required)
- Volunteering Leave: 2 days per year for approved charitable activities

Leave requests should be submitted via the HR Portal at least 2 weeks in advance for planned absences. Emergency leave can be reported retroactively within 48 hours.

4. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
-----------------------
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually in June and December. The review process includes:

- Self-assessment (submitted 2 weeks before review)
- Peer feedback (minimum 3 peers, selected by employee and manager)
- Manager evaluation and calibration
- One-on-one discussion and goal setting

Ratings scale: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory.

Employees rated "Exceeds Expectations" for two consecutive cycles are eligible for accelerated promotion consideration. Those rated "Needs Improvement" will receive a 90-day Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

5. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
------------------------------
ACME offers competitive compensation packages including:

- Base salary reviewed annually (benchmarked against industry 75th percentile)
- Annual performance bonus: 10-25% of base salary depending on level and performance
- Stock options: Granted at hire and refreshed annually for eligible roles
- 401(k) retirement plan with 6% company match
- Health insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage (employee contribution: 15%)
- Life insurance: 2x annual salary coverage
- Professional development budget: $3,000 per year for courses, conferences, certifications
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $100/month

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
-------------------
All ACME employees are expected to uphold the highest standards of professional conduct:

- Act with integrity, honesty, and respect in all business dealings.
- Maintain confidentiality of proprietary information and trade secrets.
- Report conflicts of interest to the Ethics Committee immediately.
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation.
- Compliance with all applicable local, national, and international laws.
- Responsible use of company resources (no personal use of corporate accounts).

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

7. DATA SECURITY AND PRIVACY
------------------------------
Protecting company and customer data is a top priority:

- All devices must have full-disk encryption enabled.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all internal systems.
- Sensitive data must never be transmitted over unencrypted channels.
- USB storage devices are prohibited on company-issued laptops.
- Employees must complete annual cybersecurity training (next deadline: March 31, 2025).
- Data breaches must be reported to the Security Operations Center (SOC) within 1 hour.
- Customer data is classified as CONFIDENTIAL and subject to GDPR and CCPA compliance.

8. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT
-----------------------------
ACME invests in continuous employee growth:

- ACME Academy: Internal learning platform with 5,000+ courses
- Tuition Reimbursement: Up to $10,000/year for degree programs
- Mentorship Program: Pair with senior leaders for 6-month guided development
- Hackathons: Quarterly innovation events with prizes up to $25,000
- Conference Attendance: Up to 2 sponsored conferences per year
- Certification Support: Full cost coverage for industry certifications (AWS, GCP, Azure, PMP, etc.)

9. DIVERSITY, EQUITY, AND INCLUSION (DEI)
------------------------------------------
ACME is committed to building an inclusive workplace:

- Employee Resource Groups (ERGs): 12 active ERGs including Women in Tech, Pride@ACME, Veterans Network
- Unconscious Bias Training: Mandatory for all managers, optional for ICs
- Pay Equity Audits: Conducted annually by independent third party
- Diverse Hiring Goals: 50% diverse candidate slates for all leadership positions
- Accessibility: All offices and digital products WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
